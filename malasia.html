Group tour to Uzbekistan from Almaty for the weekend 2024

Description

Group tour to Uzbekistan from Almaty, weekend 2024 Going on a group tour from Almaty to Uzbekistan, for a weekend in Tashkent and Samarkand, you enter an exciting world of adventure, full of amazing cultural and gastronomic discoveries, as well as unforgettable experiences over 4 days in the atmosphere of the ancient East.

Why go to Tashkent and Samarkand from Kazakhstan for the weekend? First of all, to see the architectural treasures of the two capitals, surrounded by mountains, where the breath of past centuries can be heard and the blue domes of ancient mosques, covered with legends, shine. The famous Registan will appear before you on the second day of the program - this is a meeting with the history of the greatness of the influential Central Asian state in the Middle Ages.

The porgram includes:

• Accommodation in hotels in double standard rooms with breakfast

• Meals: only breakfasts in hotels

• Comfortable transport with air conditioning for the entire route

- 2 pax - sedan for 3 seats

- 4-7 pax - minivan with 10 seats

- 8-20 pax - Midibus Golden Dragon

• Meeting/seeing off at the airport

• Services of local Russian-speaking guides for excursions in each city according to the program

The program doesnt include:

• International flights

• Entrance tickets to the specified monuments - paid on the spot (about 55 USD / per person)

• Alcoholic and soft drinks

• Meals (lunch and dinner)

• Fees for photo and video filming in museums

• Tipping and porters at hotels/airports

• Personal expenses

• Other services not listed above
