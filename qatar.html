Wine tour to Uzbekistan 2024 from Moscow "Wine Roads of the East"

  1. Home
  2. Tour to Uzbekistan

Description

Wine tour to Uzbekistan 2024 from Moscow “Wine Roads of the East” - a colorful journey into the world of original tastes, rich gastronomy and aromatic wines! In the fertile conditions of our country, with a unique climate, 50 varieties of grapes are grown - these characteristics are transferred to the wine, which makes the products of each region unique. The tour is available throughout the year, but the most impressive views will be found in spring and autumn. The best wineries of Uzbekistan will introduce their products and offer wine tastings - from classic, exhibition samples put into commercial production, to unique, signature collections that are grown in miniature family wineries.

Wine tour of Uzbekistan 2024 from Moscow will give amazing discoveries to gourmets and experts, and will complement an exciting excursion holiday in the cities of the Great Silk Road: Tashkent, Samarkand and Bukhara, combining the beauty of ancient architecture with the creations of nature, and immersion in a colorful culture. Visit the vineyards, see the multi-stage birth of wine, learn the nuances and subtleties.

The program includes:

✔️ Accommodation in hotels with double occupancy (registration of accommodation).

✔️ Meals: breakfasts, lunches and dinners according to the program. The names of the catering locations will be announced before the group departs.

✔️ Comfortable air-conditioned transport for the entire route

✔️ Meeting/seeing off at the airport

✔️Wine tastings and visits to wineries according to the program

✔️ Economy class tickets for the Afrosiyab train or similar, subject to availability

✔️ Tours of monuments in Russian

✔️ Entrance tickets to specified monuments

✔️ Accompanying guide throughout the tour

✔️ Good mood and excellent service

The program doesnt include:

❌ International Air Ticket

❌ Alcoholic drinks for breakfast, lunch and dinner, as well as alcohol in excess of the tasting set limit

❌ Cost and visa application (if not citizens of the Russian Federation)

❌ Fees for photo and video filming in museums

❌ Tips and porters at hotels/airports

❌ Personal expenses

❌ Other services not listed above
