Tours to Samarkand: one-day excursions from Tashkent 2024

  1. Home
  2. Tours to Samarkand

Description

Samarkand is a city of revived azure dreams, bewitching from the first moment. For 2769 years now, "Sapphire of Central Asia" has honorably met the unspoken requirements of Eternity for ancient cities - to be strong and rebellious to the will of enemies and to carefully raise the descendants of the Moroccans. To shelter, among other things, from the heat of the southern sun of Uzbekistan and the crimson sands of Kyzylkum, although, wandering through the labyrinths of cornflower blue mausoleums, it is difficult to even imagine how alarmingly close the desert opened its arms.

Excursions to Samarkand from Moscow 2024, St. Petersburg and other Russian cities are available in the following formats:

- arrival in Tashkent or Bukhara with further transfer to Samarkand by high-speed train “Afrosiab”

- start from the hotel lobby if you are already in Samarkand.

A tour to Samarkand from Moscow 2024 or Tashkent will give you an eventful and educational day, allowing you to learn the true history of the ancient city, feel its spirit and enjoy the beauty that surpasses imagination.

The cost of the program includes:

✔️ Entrance tickets to specified attractions;

✔️ Russian-speaking city guide on excursions according to the program;

✔️ Comfortable air-conditioned transport for the entire excursion route

- 1-2 people - Chevrolet Lacetti/Cobalt sedan;

- 3-10 people - Minibus (up to 10 people);

✔️ Tickets for the Economy class train "Afrosiab" Tashkent - Samarkand - Tashkent;

✔️ Transfer to the railway station of Tashkent, Samarkand and back.

The cost of the program does NOT include:

❌ Meals: Lunch and dinner; (About $9-12 per person, set menu);

❌ Accompanying guide;

❌ Tips, payment for porter services at hotels/airports/train stations;

❌ Fee for photo and video shooting in museums;

❌ Personal travel expenses;

❌ Other services not mentioned above.
